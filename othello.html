Othello.py

The game Othello, also known as Reversi, is a board game where two players try to get as many as their pieces in the board. One player plays the black pieces and another the white ones. Taking turns, a player puts a one of his pieces in the board, trying to flip the other player's pieces into his own.

If you want to learn more about the details of the game check out this wikipedia page:

Wiki Page

I coded the game in python, implementing Artificial Intelligence through a Minimax Algorithm with Alpha-Beta pruning.
